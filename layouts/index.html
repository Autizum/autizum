Autizum: A Clinician-Owned and Operated Haven for Autism Care

At Autizum, we are dedicated to providing compassionate, research-driven therapy for children with autism. As a clinician-owned and operated organization, we understand the importance of delivering high-quality, individualized care that makes a lasting impact.

Our team of experienced Board-Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) collaborates closely with families to design customized treatment plans that focus on enhancing social, communication, and daily living skills. We believe in empowering children to overcome challenges, build confidence, and develop the tools they need to thrive in their everyday lives.

Our clinician-owned and operated model allows us to have a deep understanding of the unique needs of children with autism and their families. We are able to provide personalized care that is tailored to each child's individual strengths and areas for growth.

We work closely with families, ensuring they are an integral part of the therapy process. Together, we establish goals and strategies that extend beyond therapy sessions, seamlessly integrating into daily routines.

At Autizum, we are committed to providing comprehensive skill development that focuses on enhancing communication, social interaction, and essential life skills. We understand how important it is for children with autism to build meaningful connections, improve communication, develop independence, and strengthen relationships.

Our mission is to support each child's growth and development through innovative and effective care. We are dedicated to empowering children with autism to reach their full potential and thrive in their personal and social environments.
