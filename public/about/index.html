Welcome to Autizum

Welcome to Autizum

Clients are Happy, Cause Proper Tailored Plans

At Autizum, we are a well recognized international team helping children with autism reach their full potential through personalized Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) therapy. Our team of experienced Board Certified Behaviour Analysts (BCBA) & Registered Behavior Technicians (RBT) experts collaborate closely with families to design customized treatment plans, focusing on enhancing social, communication, and daily living skills. We believe in empowering children to overcome challenges, build confidence, and develop the tools they need to thrive in their everyday lives. Our mission is to provide meaningful support, helping each child achieve lasting progress and success. Let us be a part of your child's journey toward skill acquisition such as social skills, communication in the context and building and maintaining friendship.

20 Years Professional Experience

At Autizum, our compassionate team of expert Board Certified Behaviour Analysts (BCBAs) and trained Registered Behaviour Technicians (RBTs) provide personalized, research-based ABA therapy both in-home and in-clinic. We focus on fostering your child’s development in natural, everyday environments, ensuring they grow in the areas that matter most. A key focus of our therapy is social and life skills development. We understand how important it is for your child to feel confident in social interactions and daily tasks. Our tailored programs help children build meaningful connections, improve communication, develop independence, and strengthen relationships. Our goal is to equip your child with the tools they need to thrive both socially and in everyday life, now and in the future. Let us support your family in this journey.

Mission

At Autizum, our mission is to empower children with autism by providing personalized, evidence-based Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy. We are committed to collaborating closely with families, caregivers, and educators to develop individualized treatment plans that help children build essential life skills, improve communication, and foster independence. Through compassion, expertise, and a family-centered approach, we aim to support each child in achieving meaningful, long-lasting progress.

Vision

Our vision is to be a global leader in autism care and school consultancy, recognized for delivering exceptional ABA services that transform lives. We strive to create a world where every child with autism has the opportunity to reach their full potential in a nurturing, inclusive environment. By continuously innovating our practices and expanding our reach, we aim to set new standards for comprehensive care and advocacy in the autism community, ensuring that every child has the support they need to thrive.

Our Commitment to Families and Child Development

We enhance our evaluations and treatment plans by actively including recommendations and feedback from families, caregivers, and teachers. We prioritize supporting families during transitions and helping them achieve their children's developmental goals. Our clinicians coach parents on implementing carryover interventions alongside regular care plans. We provide coaching and training in child development for community members and childcare providers. By aligning a child's environment with their needs, we foster a supportive space for them to thrive.

Company Information & About Us

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy is a scientifically validated approach that focuses on improving specific behaviors and skills in individuals with autism. Through personalized interventions, ABA therapy aims to enhance communication, social skills, and daily living skills. Our team at Autizum tailors each treatment plan to address your child's unique strengths and challenges, empowering them to reach their full potential.
At Autizum, we understand that every child is unique. Our process begins with a comprehensive assessment that includes input from families, caregivers, and teachers. We then collaborate with our multidisciplinary team of Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs) and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBTs) to develop individualized ABA therapy plans tailored to meet each child's specific needs. This collaborative approach ensures that we address your child's strengths and areas for growth effectively.
At Autizum, we focus on essential skills for children with autism, including social skills, communication, daily living, emotional development, and problem-solving. Our comprehensive approach empowers children to build friendships, express themselves, gain independence, regulate emotions, and develop critical thinking, equipping them to thrive in everyday life.
At Autizum, we prioritize family involvement in the therapy process. Our clinicians provide ongoing coaching and training to parents and caregivers, helping them implement strategies that support their child’s development at home. We also include family feedback in our evaluations and treatment plans, ensuring a collaborative approach that aligns with the child’s needs. Our goal is to empower families to be active participants in their child’s growth and progress.
Autizum stands out due to our commitment to delivering high-quality, personalized ABA therapy through a culturally sensitive and multilingual approach. Our internationally recognized team includes experts in various disciplines, allowing us to create comprehensive treatment plans that address diverse needs. We focus on building strong partnerships with families, ensuring that each child's therapy extends into their everyday life. By combining expertise, compassion, and a family-centered philosophy, we strive to make a meaningful impact on the lives of children with autism and their families.

Autizum provides individualized ABA therapy for children with autism, focusing on skill development and family collaboration.
