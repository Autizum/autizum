FLORINA DAVID COLAR

M.A., M.S., BCBA, LBA, QBA, BCASE

Licensed Behaviour Analyst in NC and VA

As the CEO and Founder of Autizum, Florina David Colar's mission is to enhance educational outcomes through expert behavior analysis and individualized support for school districts. With a focus on evidence-based practices, her team and she have established impactful social skills programs and comprehensive guidelines for international internships in behavior analysis.

Her involvement with the Babes-Bolyai University Alumni Association underscores her commitment to global mentorship, where she facilitates behavior analysis training and supports ABA practitioners. These roles leverage her skills in mentoring and clinical consulting, reflecting her dedication to advancing the field of applied behavior sciences and fostering transformative leadership within the educational community.

Born in Transylvania, Romania, Florina's journey began in Europe, where she earned her B.S. in Clinical Psychology from Babes-Bolyai State University. She later pursued her M.A. in Clinical Psychology with an emphasis in Applied Behavior Analysis at The Chicago School of Professional Psychology in the USA. Her academic pursuits were followed by extensive work experience in the UK, USA, and Canada, solidifying her expertise.

Her work in renowned institutions, such as City of Hope Medical Center in California, and her collaboration with esteemed professionals like Dr. Joyce Tu-BCBA-D and Dr. Hank Schlinger, PhD, BCBA-D, have been instrumental in shaping her approach. Florina's experience as a Behavior Consultant and Clinical Director in various agencies has equipped her with a unique understanding of the complexities of autism.

As the founder of Autizum, Florina has created a haven for high-functioning individuals with autism in California. Her expertise extends to verbal behavior programming, early intervention, and multidisciplinary evaluations. Her dedication to professional development has led to numerous publications and presentations at international conferences.

As the CEO and Founder of Autizum, Florina's mission is to enhance educational outcomes through expert behavior analysis and individualized support for school districts. Florina's personal touch and commitment to empathy have earned her the respect of families and professionals alike. Her work transcends borders, inspiring a global community to embrace inclusivity and understanding. As a Ph.D. student in Clinical Psychology, Florina continues to push the boundaries of innovation, driven by her passion for making a meaningful difference. In her spare time, Florina enjoys tennis, travel, exploring the beauty of nature with her family. She is a proud mother and a dog lover.

Florina David Colar is a shining example of dedication, compassion, and expertise. Her international appeal and commitment to advancing the field of ABA make her a beacon of hope for individuals with autism and their families worldwide.

Choosing Autizum means

Partnering with a team that is invested in your child’s success and well-being, every step of the way.

  • Our multidisciplinary team of Autizum, Speech Pathologists, Licensed Clinical Social Workers, Certified Psychologists, and Registered Behavior Technicians brings global experience and a deep understanding of diverse needs.

  • We believe that every child is unique. Our approach centers around individualized Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy, designed specifically to address each child's strengths and areas for growth.

  • We work hand-in-hand with families, ensuring they are an integral part of the therapy process. Together, we develop goals and strategies that extend beyond therapy sessions into daily routines.

  • Understanding and respecting cultural differences is at the core of our care. Our team includes multilingual clinicians who are fluent in languages such as Spanish, French, Romanian, and Arabic, allowing us to provide culturally responsive services that meet the unique needs of diverse families.

  • Our programs focus on enhancing communication, social interaction, and everyday life skills. Whether it's building friendships or improving self-care, we provide the tools children need to succeed in their personal and social environments.

  • As a clinician-owned and operated organization, we are committed to delivering compassionate, research-driven therapy that makes a lasting difference. Our mission is to support each child’s growth and development through innovative, effective care.

Autizum provides individualized ABA therapy for children with autism, focusing on skill development and family collaboration.
